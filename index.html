About Me

Meet Chase, an ambitious and driven individual who has faced challenges head-on and emerged victorious. In 2014, Chase experienced a life-changing injury that forced them to shift gears and change careers. Rather than giving up, Chase took the leap and started his own business with his brother. As Chase built their business, they realized that building apps to support their work was costly and they needed to learn to do it themselves. Driven by their determination to succeed, Chase dove headfirst into the world of programming, enrolling in a coding bootcamp. They took quickly to HTML and CSS but realized that they needed to expand their skillset and began to explore the world of JavaScript. Though new to the language, Chase is eager to learn. Off the clock, Chase enjoys spending time with friends and family, pursuing their passion for travel, and exploring new hobbies. Their determination and positive attitude make them a joy to be around, and they approach every challenge with a smile and a can-do attitude.
